Tabel Users
- user_id (primary key)
- username (text UNIQUE NOT NULL)
- password (text NOT NULL)
- email (text UNIQUE NOT NULL) 
- whatsapp(text UNIQUE NOT NULL)
- status (enum: active/pending/reject NOT NULL)
[Rencananya mau buat regis pake nomor WA biar bisa login langsung]
[Bisa pesen ruangan setelah email diaktivasi]


Tabel Rooms
- room_id (primary key)
- room name (text, UNIQUE NOT NULL)
- room building (text)
- room photo (?)
- room description (text)
- availability (enum: available/maintanance< NOT NULL)

Tabel Forms
- form_id (primary key)
- room_id (NOT NULL)
- Nama kegiatan (text NOT NULL)
- Jumlah Peserta (int NOT NULL)
- identitas peminjam (enum: FTUI/NonFTUI/Dosen NOT NULL)
- FTUI: Surat Izin Kegiatan (SIK) (pdf UNIQUE NOT NULL)
- Non-FTUI: Surat Dekan FTUI (pdf UNIQUE NOT NULL)
- Fasilitas yang dibutuhkan beserta jumlahnya (text)
- konsumsi (ada/tidak) (bool NOT NULL)

Tabel Books
- book_id (primary key)
- user_id (NOT NULL)
- form_id (UNIQUE NOT NULL)
- time (timestamp NOT NULL)
- book_date (date NOT NULL)
- book_time_start (time NOT NULL)
- book_duration (int NOT NULL)
